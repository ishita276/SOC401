A Student Perspective

As an international student navigating academic pressure, cultural adjustment, and limited mental-health resources, I learned firsthand how digital platforms can become both a coping mechanism and a source of distress.

What Different Actors Can Do

Federal Policymakers

  • Create a Youth Digital Well-Being Data Initiative.
  • Enforce youth-focused privacy and safety standards.

Schools & Universities

  • Track counseling wait times.
  • Provide culturally competent care.

Parents & Communities

  • Engage in open conversations about digital well-being.

Students

  • Share personal experiences.
  • Advocate for safer, healthier online spaces.
