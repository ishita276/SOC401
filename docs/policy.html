Policy Options

1. Platform Risk Assessments

  • Requires platforms to assess harms.
  • Increases transparency.

2. Youth-Centered Design Requirements

  • High-privacy defaults.
  • Limits on addictive features.

3. Federal Youth Digital Well-Being Data Initiative (Recommended)

  • Standardizes youth digital well-being indicators.
  • Allows privacy-protective data sharing with platforms.
  • Creates real-time dashboards and early warning systems.

Why This Is Preferred

Because we cannot regulate what we cannot measure. Better data enables policymakers, schools, and communities to respond quickly and effectively.
